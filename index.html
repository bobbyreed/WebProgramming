📚 Course Overview

Welcome to the course! This course serves as an introduction to creating and maintaining mostly frontend web technologies.

We'll be exploring HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git/GitHub, and modern web development practices. The course is designed to give you practical, hands-on experience building real websites from scratch.
